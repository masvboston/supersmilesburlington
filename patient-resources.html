Initial visit

Your initial visit will consist of a comprehensive dental evaluation. Dr. Miller will review your medical history, explain your dental diagnosis and present your treatment options. Initial dental evaluations are commonly completed with a full set of dental xrays. You may request a set of your xrays to be forwarded to our office if they were taken within the last six months. If additional films are necessary, they can be taken at our facility.

We appreciate your assistance in providing us with the following information on the day of your appointment:

  • Xrays (if applicable)
  • A list of your current medical prescriptions.
    Please inform our office if you have any medical conditions that may be of concern prior to dental treatment (e.g. high blood pressure, previous heart surgery, allergies to medicine, etc.)
  • Your dental insurance information

Treatment

After your initial visit, you will schedule an appointment on another day for any procedure you may need according to your treatment plan. Occasionally, treatment can be done on the same day as the initial visit if there is enough time allowed.

Emergency/Problem Focused Visit

Should you experience pain or have an emergency situation, we will make every effort to see you that day. Dr. Miller may prescribe one or two xrays of the area in question to help with the diagnosis. If time allows, Dr. Miller may provide treatment immediately to either provide an immediate remedy or mitigate your symptoms until your follow-up appointment when more time is available to provide a comprehensive solution.

If you do not have dental insurance or if your dental insurance plan does not cover any part of your dental treatment, we can help you identify the financing options available to you, and discuss which may be most appropriate for your particular needs.
