Dr. Emilie Miller, DMD

Dr. Miller practiced in several locations in Massachusetts before establishing her own dental practice in Burlington, MA. Dr. Miller graduated Cum Laude from Boston University Goldman School of Dentistry in 2005 and started her dental career in under-served communities in Roxbury and Dorchester. In 2006, she was appointed to the position of Director of the Dental Department at Geiger-Gibson Community Health Center. During her two year directorship, Dr. Miller also taught students of dental medicine on their externship rotation, earning the title of Assistant Clinical Professor at Boston University Goldman School of Dentistry and Harvard School of Dental Medicine.

Dr. Miller keeps her skills and knowledge current on advancements in general dentistry and dental technologies by regularly attending seminars that involve multidisciplinary treatment planning. She is an active member of the Massachusetts Dental Society and the American Dental Association.

Dr. Miller was born and raised in Montreal, Canada, and is fluent in both English and French. In her spare time, she enjoys reading, hiking the White Mountains in New Hampshire, ballroom dancing and getting her beloved husband fat by baking delicious desserts. Her pet peeve is proper spelling.

Patient Care Coordinator Anne-Marie Sherzi

With over 30 years of experience, Ms. Sherzi is an expert Patient Care Coordinator. Ms. Sherzi absolutely loves her profession and it shows.

Head Assistant Jennifer Hanson

Ms. Hanson is a veteran dental professional with over 20 years of experience as a Dental Assistant and Patient Coordinator. Ms. Hanson is a highly trained chair-side assistant and keeps abreast of the latest advancements in dental care and technology through continuing education classes and skill-focused training; a commitment she takes very seriously. Ms. Hanson has a very gentle and compassionate chair-side manner and ensures patients receive the best and most informative treatment possible.

"My favourite part of this job is changing people's opinions about going to the dentist and getting them excited to come in for their visits."

Ms. Hanson is originally from East Boston and now lives in Beverly with her beloved husband, 2 children, and 2 year old beagle. In her spare time she loves to read, especially to her children, and volunteers at the school library. Her pet peeve is disorder.
